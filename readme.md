# Created for my Mother.

There are a couple of signs in town. She updates these signs for the township.
She used to do this all by hand. Writing down what was currently written, then doing the math for what was needed to bring from her inventory, what could be swapped, and what needs to be removed. Then she would go to the sign and update it.

My first solution was a spreadsheet. However, after a while of use, this corrupted.
